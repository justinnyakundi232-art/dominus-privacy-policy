Dominus Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

Dominus does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

How Dominus Works

Dominus processes website URLs locally on the user's device solely to determine whether a website matches the user's configured block list.

Data Storage

Blocked sites, categories, and user settings are stored locally using Chrome's storage functionality.

Third Parties

No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Contact

Contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
